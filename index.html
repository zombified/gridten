  • force go down: 3, w, up arrow
  • force go left: 4, w, up arrow
  • insta kill: 5, q, '/'
  • Mute 'm'
  • reset the game: 'r'
Some rules:
  • Click on an intersection to activate it, then click a key for an object to add it.
  • If the cyan dots are forced to the edge of the board, they die
  • Every 10 seconds, your score goes up. It also goes up (just a little bit) when a cyan dot dies
  • The score is used to determine how many points can be spent on objects. If your score falls below an objects point cost, you cannot place that object.
  • The game ends when the green circle is hit by snakes 10 times.
